Remarkable moments of Ayrton Senna's Formula One career

Mar 21, 1960 - Ayrton Senna was born in São Paulo, Brazil.
Jan 1, 1964 - He started carting.
Jan 2, 1981 - Ayrton Senna left school and moved to England for racing.
Oct 2, 1981 - He won 13 of 18 formula ford racing.
Oct 2, 1982 - He bacame the champion of Formula Ford 2000.
Oct 2, 1983 - He became the champion of British Formula 3 racing.
Mar 2, 1984 - He started to race in Formula 1.
Oct 2, 1988 - He joined Frenchman Alain Prost at McLaren-Honda.
Oct 2, 1990 - Senna bacame champion of Formula 1.
Oct 2, 1991 - Senna bacame 2x champion of Formula 1.
Oct 2, 1991 - Senna bacame 3x champion of Formula 1.
May 1, 1994 - Senna passed away due to an accident during Italian Grand Prix.
